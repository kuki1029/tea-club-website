About Us
We are UW's finest Tea Club! We meet every week Wednesdays in EV2 2002 at 6-8 pm. Every week we have 4 different teas and unlimited snacks and fun board games. Come along and make new friends, or do your assignment, or read a book or whatever you want as long as you're drinking tea! P.S. Once a month we have special snacks from a local bakery ;)
Membership
The easier way to get a membership is to just show up to our meetings! We charge $20 for the term or $4 for each session. The term membership also comes with other special benefits! You can also purchase a membership by clicking here.
